Site Plan - São Paulo Futebol Clube

Site Name

São Paulo Futebol Clube Fan Site

This is not the official website of São Paulo Futebol Clube,I will providing all necessary information about the club, team details, and match schedules.

Site Purpose

The site serves as the official hub for São Paulo Futebol Clube, offering team rosters, match schedules and a platform for of the club.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest news and updates about São Paulo FC?
  • Where can I find the schedule of upcoming matches?
  • information about the players
  • The team History
  • A gallery with stadium images

Color Schema

The color scheme for the site will be based on the official colors of São Paulo Futebol Clube:

  • Red (#e01c29): Used for headings and accents.
  • Black (#000000): Used for body text and borders.
  • White (#ffffff): Used for some body text.

Typography

The following fonts will be used on the website:

  • Arial: Used for body text for readability.
  • Helvetica: Used for headings to create a modern and clean look.
